# College of Attralucian Studies

**School of Geofinitism** | [geofinitism-core](https://github.com/Geofinitism/geofinitism-core)

> *Symbolic, narrative, and emergent dimensions — creative and exploratory Geofinitism.*

The College of Attralucian Studies is the creative and exploratory wing of the School of Geofinitism. It houses essays that approach Geofinitism through metaphor, narrative, comparative philosophy, and emergent symbolic form. Where other colleges formalise, Attralucian Studies explores — tracing the basin from the inside.
