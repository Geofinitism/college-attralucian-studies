## The Problem with Assumed Time

All classical accounts share a common move: time is placed in the background and left unexamined.

- **Aristotle**: time is the measure of change — but what measures time itself?
- **Newton**: time is absolute, flowing uniformly without relation to anything external — a perfect infinite backdrop
- **Einstein**: time is relative to velocity and gravity — but still a continuous, differentiable parameter

In each case, time is *assumed* rather than *constructed*. It arrives before measurement, not from it.

Geofinitism rejects this inheritance:

> If all measurement is finite, and all symbols arise through generonic processes, then time must itself be constructed.

## Building Blocks

### The Generon

A **generon** is a minimal act of stable distinction — the smallest unit of symbolic formation. To distinguish A from B requires compression: the interaction must be stabilised into a finite, admissible form. This stabilisation has a cost.

### Distinction Cost

Every generonic act introduces:

**C(D) > 0**

No distinction is free. All stabilisations carry compression cost and uncertainty.

### The Alphonic Limit

The smallest admissible distinction is bounded below:

**δ_α > 0**

Below this limit, no distinction exists. Time has a ground floor — it cannot be infinitely subdivided.

---

## ~Time: The Formula

Geofinite time is the ordered accumulation of distinction costs:

**~τ_α = C(D_min)** — the minimal temporal unit (one alphonic distinction)

**~T = Σ C(G_{i+1} − G_i)** — time as the sum of successive generonic transitions

~Time is therefore:
- **Discrete** — built from countable generonic acts
- **Cumulative** — each distinction adds to the total
- **Bounded** — no infinite divisibility; the alphonic limit holds
- **Emergent** — not a background, but a consequence of ordered compression

Classical time T is a projection of this — a smooth interpolation that suppresses the underlying discreteness. Useful, but not fundamental.

## The Tilde Notation

Throughout this essay and forward in the series, the tilde (~) marks Geofinite quantities:

| Notation | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| T | Classical continuous time (assumed) |
| ~T | Geofinite time (constructed from generonic compression) |

The tilde is a **boundary marker**. It signals:

> This quantity is not assumed. It is constructed.

It resists **symbolic lift** — the classical move of taking a finite measurement and placing it on an infinite, assumed structure (the real number line). The real number line is not measured; it is asserted. The tilde stays grounded.

---

## Symbolic Lift

Symbolic lift is the inadmissible move:

1. A symbol is introduced (e.g., a time measurement)
2. It is placed on a number line
3. The number line is assumed to be infinite and continuous

This is extremely common in classical physics and mathematics. It works as a useful fiction — but Geofinitism insists on marking the boundary where the finite measurement ends and the assumed infinite structure begins.

The tilde does this marking.

## Biological Stabilisation and Lived Time

Not all generonic distinctions persist. Distinctions decay unless reinforced. Memory is selective stabilisation.

This gives two layers of ~Time:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Generonic ~Time** | All distinctions — the full accumulation |
| **Experienced ~Time** | Retained and reinforced distinctions — the filtered record |

Lived time is not just constructed; it is filtered. This connects the formal model to cognition, memory, and the phenomenology of duration — without requiring any infinite substrate.
