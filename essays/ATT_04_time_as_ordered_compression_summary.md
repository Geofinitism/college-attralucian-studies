## Core trajectory

This essay reframes time from an assumed primitive dimension into a constructed, emergent quantity — the ordered accumulation of compressed distinctions (generonic transitions) within a finite symbolic system — situating this formulation within the history of temporal thought and introducing the tilde notation to mark all Geofinite quantities as constructed rather than assumed.

## Key claim

~Time is not fundamental but emergent: it equals the ordered sum of distinction costs across generonic transitions (~T = Σ C(G_{i+1} − G_i)), making time finite, discrete, cumulative, and bounded — and classical continuous time a useful projection that suppresses underlying discreteness.

## Key evidence

- Formal definition: ~τ_α = C(D_min); ~T = Σ C(G_{i+1} − G_i)
- Alphonic limit δ_α > 0: below this, no distinction exists — time has a ground floor
- **Symbolic lift** — the classical error of introducing a finite symbol and then placing it on an assumed infinite number line; the tilde resists this move
- Aristotle → Newton → Einstein: each treats time as assumed; Geofinitism breaks this inheritance
- Biological stabilisation: memory is selective stabilisation; experienced ~Time is filtered as well as constructed
- The Geofinite continuum is not a structure but a potential — the capacity for further distinction, not infinite divisibility

| **Generon** | A minimal act of stable distinction; the foundational unit of symbolic formation |
