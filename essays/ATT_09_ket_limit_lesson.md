## The Ket Limit

Standard quantum mechanics describes a system via a state vector |ψ⟩ in an infinite-dimensional Hilbert space. Constants like π are treated as exact Platonic ideals. Yet all empirical access is via finite measurements — a stream of symbols from a finite alphabet (an **Alphon**), with bounded resolution.

The **Ket Limit** is the epistemic boundary:

**Resolution(|ψ⟩) > ε_total = max(U_ex, 1/b, L/N^(1/d_P))**

where:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| U_ex | Exogenous uncertainty — the physical measurement limit |
| b | Alphabet size — the symbolic resolution |
| N | Number of measurement samples |
| d_P | Platonic (ideal) dimension of the system |
| L | Characteristic scale |

Beyond this limit, |ψ⟩ is not a description of reality. It is a Platonic projection — a map that has mistaken itself for the territory. Wavefunction collapse and quantum weirdness are artefacts of this over-extension, not physical phenomena requiring metaphysical explanation.

## The Geofinitist Resolution Bound (GRB)

The GRB formalises the hard bound on model precision from three independent sources:

**ε_total ≥ max(U_ex, 1/b, L/N^(1/d_P))**

The bound is determined by whichever constraint is hardest. No refinement of the other two can overcome the limiting one. This is the operative consequence of Pillar 2 (Approximations and Measurements) applied to physics: every model has an honest precision ceiling, and pretending otherwise produces pathologies.

## The Exponential Demystified

The exponential appears everywhere in quantum mechanics: unitary evolution e^{−iHt/ℏ}, Boltzmann factors e^{−βε_j}, partition functions. It has the appearance of a fundamental Platonic entity.

Geofinitism grounds it in finite combinatorics: count the arrangements of N finite resources among M states under constraint. The most probable distribution takes the form:

**p_j ≈ (1/Z) e^{−βε_j} ± Δp_j**

The uncertainty Δp_j is **constitutive** — it is not a flaw but the honest signature of finite counting. The infinite-precision exponential is the Δp_j → 0 limit: a useful fiction, not the reality.

**Principle of Exponential Emergence**: the exponential is the semantic shape of finite multiplicative processes observed through linear symbolic systems. Its universality is explained — it is not a mystery of nature but a mathematical inevitability of counting.

## The Circular Uncertainty Distribution (CUD)

The Gaussian is built for an infinite world: it has infinite support, infinite tails, and infinite precision parameters. It is a useful fiction — but the wrong native object for a finite framework.

The **CUD** is the honest replacement:

| Property | CUD | Gaussian |
|----------|-----|---------|
| Support | Finite — circle of circumference M = ⌊π·b⌋ | Infinite — real line |
| Tails | None — hard boundary | Infinite — asymptotically approach zero |
| Parameters | Measurable numbers with explicit ±U_ex | Implicitly infinite precision |
| Probability floor | E_min = 1/(bN) — smallest representable value | Arbitrarily small (no floor) |

The CUD is not an approximation of the Gaussian. It is a **fundamentally different object** that reduces to the Gaussian only in the unphysical infinite limit. It is the native statistical object of a finite world.

## The Finite Turn Applied

### Hydrogen Atom
The stable orbital is not a planetary orbit or a probability cloud. It is a **closed geodesic on the reconstructed attractor manifold**. Stability is a geometric inevitability — the trajectory naturally closes on itself — not a quantised law requiring mysterious imposition.

### Quantum Statistics
Fermi-Dirac and Bose-Einstein distributions are reformulated with measurable numbers and approximate zeros. The Pauli Exclusion Principle becomes **approximate** at finite resolution. Bose-Einstein condensation becomes a smooth crossover, not an infinitely sharp phase transition.

### Singularities and Quantum Gravity
Spacetime is a finite symbolic manifold. The Planck scale ℓ_P is its fundamental alphabet. The concepts of infinite density — black hole singularities, the Big Bang — are replaced by finite regions of size ~ℓ_P where density, while immense, is finite. **Singularities vanish not by fiat but as a natural consequence of finite geometry.**

This provides a path to finite quantum gravity on the common ground of finite interaction — the same manifold framework that handles language and LLMs also handles spacetime.
